Wiener melange.




  • Melange is popular in Austria, Switzerland and the Netherlands, and is traditionally similar to latte and cappuccino.

  • It consists of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam served in a large cup. It is sometimes topped with whipped cream.

  • A Wiener Melange is a speciality coffee drink similar to a cappuccino. The difference is sometimes assumed to be that
    the melange is made with milder coffee.
